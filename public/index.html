What this is

Most of what goes wrong at work is the system, not the people in it. You answer ten concrete questions about how your workplace actually runs. Your answers join everyone else's — weighted, aggregated, and published in weekly batches.

No account needed. No individual rating is ever shown. Nothing displays below 5 raters. There are no rankings here — workplaces are compared to the expected range, not to each other.

Why it works

"It's just me" is what every broken system wants you to believe. Numbers at scale say otherwise: that's not a you problem — that's a system problem. And systems, unlike people, can be fixed on purpose.

The rules we can't break

No ads. No trackers. No selling data. No paid influence on any score. No leaderboards. The math is public at /v1/methodology; the mission is measured in systems improved, not clicks.
