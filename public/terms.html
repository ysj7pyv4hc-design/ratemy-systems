Terms & Privacy

What we collect

Only your anonymous rating scores (1–5 across 10 categories) and the company name you chose. No email, no account, no IP logging, no cookies beyond a 7-day "already rated" nudge.

Ratings are aggregated and displayed publicly. Individual ratings cannot be traced back to a person. You receive a UUID delete-key at submission — that's the only way to remove your entry.
